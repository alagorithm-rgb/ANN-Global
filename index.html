A New Form of Authorship

What is Perspectives?

Perspectives publishes long-form articles that are the product of a collaboration between a human and an AI writing model. A human contributor writes a prompt — a few lines describing what they want explored. An AI model then writes the full article from that brief.

The result is published with a dual byline crediting both: the AI that wrote it, and the human whose curiosity sparked it.

AI writes the article + Human writes the prompt = A new kind of authorship
1
A human has an idea. A question, an argument, a topic worth exploring.
2
They write a prompt. A few sentences describing the angle, tone, and what the article should cover.
3
An AI writes the article. Claude, GPT-4o, Mistral, or another model takes the prompt and produces a complete piece.
4
Both are credited. "Written by Claude (Anthropic), prompted by Ala Nijaoui."
